Durham House of Pets

Policies

Financing:

We have several different financing options for some of our more expensive services and products which include layaway, equipment financing, zero down payments, and flexible term rates.

Delivery

We offer a variety of different delivery services such as overnight, express (2-4 business day), and standard (5-7 business days).

Returns

Any product you buy from Durham House of Pets can be returned within a 30 day time period as long as the product is in acceptable condition and a valid receipt is presented. Animals may be returned at any time to ensure that each animal has a loving and safe environment to grow, develop, and thrive.
